Privacy Policy for Strict Session Blocker

Last Updated: 10/9/2025

Strict Session Blocker is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.

Your browsing data never leaves your device.

Data Storage

All settings (such as your Allowed List) are stored locally in your browser using Chrome’s built-in storage.

No Data Collection

We do not collect or send any data to external servers.

No Third Parties

Your information is never shared with third parties.
